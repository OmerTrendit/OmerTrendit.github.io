Token Distribution and Payments


Trend Tokens will be divided into 3 main categories as shown below:




120 Million tokens will be distributed for the initial coin offering.



40 Million tokens will be used for acquiring users on Trendit.



40 Million tokens will be in reserve for employee incentives and our advisors.


Payments of Trend Tokens will be divided amongst content creators and TrenditStudios




50% of profits will be distributed to TrenditStudios to support development.



A collection of smart contracts will be used to distribute payments.



50% of profits will be paid to Trendit users based on their created content.

Crowdsale

Long Term Platform Development: 45%
Salaries: 30%

Team: 5%

Use of the raised funds will be displayed publicly to enable transparency with our investors.

Trendit Roadmap


  • Company Established SEP 2017
    TrenditStudios goals and the founding team were established!
  • Platform Design OCT 2017
    Trendit's primary functionalities and design goals were established. Also the token model and it's distribution strategy was finalized.
  • Initial Coin Offering JAN 2018
    TrenditStudios will launch a 1 month long Initial Coin Offering through a crowdsale.
  • App Development Q1 2018
    Hire an in-house development team with crowdsale funds. Complete the basic development of a web application. Start working on mobile versions of the application.
  • Beta-Launch Q2 2018
    Beta launch of the web and mobile platforms. Start letting investors and early adopters test and discover bugs on the developed applications. Pay early adopters with Trend Tokens!
  • Marketing Q3 2018
    Use marketing portion of crowdsale funds to drive users to our platform through social media interaction and mass advertising.
  • Creator Payments Q1 2019
    Start paying content creators dividends in both Ethereum and Trend Tokens. Trend Tokens will continue to be paid out until user acquisition tokens are depleted.
