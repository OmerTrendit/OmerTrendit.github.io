What is Trendit?


A social media platform that pays you in cryptocurrency to make viral content! The more viral your content, the more you earn.



Trends

Make viral content related to fashion, food, or other trendy categories such as memes. Make money based on how viral your created content becomes!

Events

Have an upcoming event or want to plan a new event? Create one on the trendit platform to bring more people to your event, all while getting paid.

Challenges

Want to challenge the world or just your city? Create a new challenge and earn money on its popularity!


ERC20 Token

Platform payments, promotions and events are linked fundamentally to our ERC20 based Cryptocurrency token. The token will also allow investors with an early access privilege to the platform. The token is built on top of the Ethereum network.

Promotions

The trendit platform gives people the opportunity to promote content to our users for a small fee paid in ethereum or trendit tokens. Money earned from promotions helps drives more earnings for our users.

Buyback

Anytime payments are made using trendit tokens on the platform we will automatically burn half the tokens used in the payment. Tokens will be continuously burned up until only 100 million remain.

Social Media Platform


Trendit's goal is to be a social media website that helps viral content get created. Making viral content isn't easy, it takes effort and planning. At trendit we provide a simple platform for users all around the world to organize and control exposure to their viral content. Not only that, we also ensure our users get paid in cryptocurrency for their efforts!

Planned Token Distribution



- There will be a total of 200 Million Trend Tokens. The goal after burning will be to limit the total circulating supply of tokens to a max of 100 Million Trend Tokens.

- 120 Million Trend Tokens will be sold through an Initial Coin Offering to investors. Investors will benefit from early access to the platform as well as a token burn incentive.

- 40 Million Trend Tokens will be used to acquire users to the Trendit platform. These tokens will be divided and paid out equally in a bi-weekly payout fashion. Users joining the platform early will see the greatest benefit here.

- The remaining 30+10 Million Trend Tokens will be reserved for Advisors, Founders and Employee incentives to reward rapid development of the platform. Also this reserve will be used for marketing and acquisition if required.





Revenue Payout Model



- Revenue on the Trendit platform will be earned through advertisements, promoted content, and competitions.

- The revenue earned on the platform will be managed completely by a smart contract forming a Decentralized Autonomous Organization. The contract will evaluate both payments in Trend Tokens as well as Ethereum.

- If payments are earned in Trend Tokens then the smart contract will check circulating supply of tokens and burn half the payment if it is made in Trend Tokens.

- The remaining revenue will be split two ways. 50% of the revenue will go to the Trendit platform while the remaining 50% will be paid to our content creators. The more viral their content the more of this share the users will earn.


Trendit Roadmap


  • Company Established SEP 2017
    Trendit studios goals and the founding team were established!
  • Platform Design Established OCT 2017
    Trendit's primary functionalities and design goals were established. Also the token model and it's distribution strategy was finalized.
  • Initial Coin Offering JAN 2018
    Trendit Studios will launch a 1 month long Initial Coin Offering through a crowdsale.
  • App Development Q1 2018
    Hire an in-house development team with crowdsale funds. Complete the basic development of a web application. Start working on mobile versions of the application.
  • Beta-Launch Q2 2018
    Beta launch of the web and mobile platforms. Start letting investors and early adopters test and discover bugs on the developed applications. Pay early adopters with Trendit Tokens!
  • Marketing Q3 2018
    Use marketing portion of crowdsale funds to drive users to our platform through social media interaction and mass advertising.
  • Creator Payments Q1 2019
    Start paying content creators dividends in Ethereum instead of Trendit Tokens. Burn any Trendit Tokens not paid out through the user acquisition bank.
