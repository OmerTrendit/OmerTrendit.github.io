What is Trendit?

A social media platform that pays you in cryptocurrency to make viral content! The more viral your content, the more you earn.


Trends

Make viral content related to fashion, food, or other trendy categories. Make money based on how viral your created content becomes!

Events

Have an upcoming event or want to plan a new event? Create one on the trendit platform to bring more people to your event, all while getting paid.

Challenges

Want to challenge the world or just your city? Create a new challenge and earn money on its popularity!


ERC20 Token

Dividend payments to our users and investors are linked fundamentally to our ERC20 based Cryptocurrency token. The token is built on top of the Ethereum network.

Promotions

The trendit platform gives people the opportunity to promote content to our users for a small fee. Money earned from promotions helps drives more earnings for our users and investors.

Dividends

Based on our quarterly earnings, trendit studios will be paying our token holders as well as content creators a 50% cut of our profits.

Social Media Platform


Trendit's goal is to be a social media website that helps viral content get created. Making viral content isn't easy, it takes effort and planning. At trendit we provide a simple platform for users all around the world to organize and control exposure to their viral content. Not only that, we also ensure our users get paid in cryptocurrency for their efforts!

Trendit Roadmap


  • Company Established SEP 2017
    Trendit studios goals and the founding team were established!
  • Platform Design Established OCT 2017
    Trendit's primary functionalities and design goals were established. Also the token model and it's distribution strategy was finalized.
  • Initial Coin Offering DEC 2017
    Trendit Studios will launch a 1 month crowdsale near late December.
  • App Development Q1 2018
    Hire an in-house development team with crowdsale funds. Complete the basic development of a web application. Start working on mobile versions of the application.
  • Beta-Launch Q2 2018
    Beta launch of the web and mobile platforms. Start letting investors and early adopters test and discover bugs on the developed applications.
  • Marketing Q3 2018
    Use marketing portion of crowdsale funds to drive users to our platform through social media interaction and mass advertising. Start paying early adopters/content creators with Trendit Tokens.
  • Dividend Payments Q2 2018
    Start payout of dividends to token holders! Start paying content creators dividends in Ethereum instead of Trendit Tokens. Burn any Trendit Tokens not paid out through the user acquisition bank.
